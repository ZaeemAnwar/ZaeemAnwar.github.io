FAQ

Answers parents and students usually ask first.

Who is the typical student?

Parents reach out for learners about 12–18. University students often contact me themselves for coursework and skill-building.

Online or in person?

Sessions are in person within roughly 20 km of Aurora (e.g. Newmarket, Richmond Hill north, nearby). The free intro is by phone or video — whichever we agree on.

What does the $80/hr include?

One hour of focused 1:1 time, plus up to 30 minutes of asynchronous help between meetings for short questions (text or email), so momentum continues during the week.

Is there a minimum number of sessions?

No. We proceed session by session after the intro.

Where do we meet?

We can work at your home, a public library, or my location — whatever is practical and comfortable for that student and family.

How do you approach AI?

AI is part of how we work now; the goal is to use it intentionally — to learn faster, not to skip thinking. We cover when to use it, how to verify results, and how to protect fundamentals and academic integrity.
