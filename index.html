FAQ

Answers parents and students usually ask first.

Who is the typical student?

Parents reach out for learners aged 12–18. University students often contact me directly for coursework and skill-building.

Online or in person?

Sessions are in person within roughly 20 km of Aurora (e.g. Newmarket, Richmond Hill north, surrounding areas). The free intro is by phone or video.

What does the $80/hr include?

One hour of focused 1:1 time, plus up to 30 minutes of asynchronous support between sessions for short questions (text or email) so momentum continues during the week.

Is there a minimum number of sessions?

No. We go session by session after the intro.

Where do we meet?

At your home, a public library, or my location. Whatever is practical and comfortable for the student and family.

How do you approach AI?

AI is part of how we work now. The goal is to use it intentionally: to learn faster, not to skip thinking. We cover when to use it, how to verify results, and how to protect fundamentals and academic integrity.
